Meet Echo Dot

With a new speaker and design,Echo Dot is a voice-controlled smart speaker with Alexa,designed for any room. Just ask for music, news, information, and more.You can also call almost anyone, and control compatible smart home devices. Connect to other speakers with Bluetooth or a 3.5mm audio cable.
